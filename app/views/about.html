Back Story

Tyler Buchea created Lure.js to help him further understand how and why MVC frameworks had become so popular. He knew about the latest and greatest frameworks but trying to sift through their large repositories only hindered his comprehension.

Without any good examples he set out to make his own MVC not only to learn himself, but to serve as a learning tool for other programmers who want a simplified explanation written in JavaScript.
