The controllers are the functions of Lure.js they are what change the model and the way they do this is through the scope object.

Scope is a global variable that all controllers have access to. This is how data is shared throughout your app.

The model is how you structure your data within the global scope object, so that it will merge nicely with your templates. You could say that the scope is equivelant to the model.
